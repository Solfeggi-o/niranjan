Public Service

Beyond advocacy, Niranjan serves as an appointed Member of the Kolliyangunam Village Panchayat. His participation in local governance ensures that inclusion is embedded in grassroots decision-making ‚Äî not as an afterthought, but as a foundation for community well-being.

Grassroots Governance

Village Panchayats are the foundation of India's democratic system, representing the most local level of governance. By serving in this role, Niranjan brings disability justice perspectives directly into community decision-making processes, ensuring that accessibility and inclusion are considered from the ground up.

Community Impact

Through his role in the Panchayat, Niranjan demonstrates that disability representation in governance is not just about token inclusion, but about bringing essential perspectives to community planning, resource allocation, and policy implementation. His work shows that when disabled people are part of decision-making structures, communities become more accessible and inclusive for everyone.
