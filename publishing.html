Inclusive Publishing

Niranjan is the founder of Inclusive Dhamma Publications, a platform dedicated to amplifying marginalized and underrepresented voices ‚Äî including persons with disabilities, women, transgender, and Dalit communities.

Mission

Inclusive Dhamma Publications challenges the traditional publishing landscape by centering voices that have historically been excluded. The platform recognizes that knowledge justice requires not just access to information, but the amplification of diverse perspectives and lived experiences.

Recognition

Niranjan's commitment to inclusive publishing and knowledge justice was recognized with the Dhamma Chakra Award, honoring his work in making spiritual and philosophical teachings accessible to all communities.

Impact

Through Inclusive Dhamma Publications, Niranjan creates space for stories, teachings, and perspectives that have been marginalized. This work is part of a broader commitment to ensuring that disability justice, gender justice, caste justice, and other forms of social justice are not separate struggles, but interconnected movements toward a more inclusive world.
