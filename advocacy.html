Leadership & Advocacy

As the Secretary of the Tamil Disability Alliance, Niranjan helps lead a collective of disabled people and allies across Tamil Nadu. The Alliance builds leadership, shapes public policy, and transforms social attitudes through collaboration and education.

Tamil Disability Alliance

The Tamil Disability Alliance represents a powerful collective voice for disability rights in Tamil Nadu.
